Momo

Description

Momo is a type of steamed filled dumpling in Tibetan and Nepali cuisine that is also popular in neighbouring Bhutan and India.Momo is usually served with a sauce known as achar influenced by the spices and herbs used within many South Asian cuisines.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Meat of any kind
  • Onion
  • Spices
  • Tomato
  • Green Chilly

Steps

  1. Take flour,water and a pinch of salt and make a dough out of it.
  2. Take small balls of the dough and make circular wrappings to place your fillings.
  3. In a separate bowl place meat, diced onions and whole lot of spices along with a bit of oil and mix it.
  4. Place a small portion of the filling inside the wrapper and close the dough.
  5. Place in the steamer for about 10 minutes
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
