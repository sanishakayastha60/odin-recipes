Momo

Description

Laping is a spicy cold mung bean noodle dish in Tibetan cuisine. It is a street food and is also popular in Nepal.It can be eaten with red pepper chili, coriander and green onion sauce.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Soy sauce
  • Wai wai
  • Spices
  • Chilli oil

Steps

  1. Take flour and wash it under cold water until gluten is separated. Don't throw the white water after the wash.
  2. Take the gluten and let it rest for about 15 minutes before steaming it.
  3. Keep aside the white water for about 4 hours. Throw the water at the top and what your are left with is starch.
  4. Take a greased plate and add the starch in a thin layer and steam it.
  5. Take a sheet made from starch then add the small bits of gluten along with chilli oil,wai wai and soy sauce. Roll it up and cut in bite size pieces.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
